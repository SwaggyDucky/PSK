Welcome to PSK

Welcome to the internet home of the secret kitchen. Being here means you are in the know. Look at some pictures, buy some merch(when in stock), give us some feedback, and maybe even check out our latest menu!

Powell's Secret Kitchen was started with a George Foreman grill, some late night munchies, and the belief that everyone deserves a delicious sandwich on the weekend when everything else is closed. We have continued to stay true to these humble beginnings, now with just a few more grills and probably the same amount of late night munchies.
